What it does · how it works

Every request leaves with one decision.

You paste the mess. BriefLock reads it, checks the scope, prices it on fixed math, and drafts the documents — four steps, and you can audit every one.

01Read

It reads the whole request

An email, a call transcript, your notes — every source, every format — pulled into one structured intake.

MethodologyReads every source, not just the first email. When a detailed transcript conflicts with a casual note, the detailed source wins.
reference/intake-schema.md
02Check

It checks the scope

A fixed checklist: what kind of build it is, how many systems it touches, whether the scope is locked or open-ended.

MethodologyToo thin to price honestly? It blocks and hands you the exact questions — never a guessed number.
rules.md
03Price

The same calculator, every time

The checklist runs through fixed math grounded in real market rates — the model picks the band and the price.

MethodologyDeterministic: same request in, same price out. You label; the engine computes. You never improvise a number.
reference/price_calc.py
04Draft

Price, model, receipt, contract

Out comes the price, the right way to charge, a receipt that shows the math, and a scope-locked proposal.

MethodologyEvery price ships with its receipt — the work, the margin, the rate, the overhead. Nothing hidden.
reference/proposal-template.html
